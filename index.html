Welcome to Crystal Collector!

Here's how to play the game

1. Under Number to Achieve, you will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystals, you will add the numbers until you get to that specific number that was given.

3. You will win if your number matches with the Number to Achieve.

4. You will lose if your number reaches a value that is higher than the Number to Achieve.
